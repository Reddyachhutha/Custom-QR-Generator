Background Image Tips 💡

Blending an image into a QR code looks amazing, but it can make it harder for cameras to scan!

How to ensure it scans perfectly:

  1. Contrast is Key: Ensure your "Code Color" and the Background Image have high contrast. The smart contrast tool helps, but extreme colors work best.
  2. Avoid Clutter: Very busy images might confuse scanners. Simple gradients or clean textures are ideal.
  3. Keep Eyes Clear: The 3 large corner squares are automatically forced solid to guarantee scanning.
  4. Test It: Always test scan the generated QR code with your phone before sharing it!
